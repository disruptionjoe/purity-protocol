# Purity Protocol Decisions

This is the authoritative record of ratified decisions and constitutional
amendments. Entries are append-only in meaning: later entries may supersede
earlier ones only by naming them and preserving history.

## Decision method

Every entry must include an identifier, date, class, authority, status, scope,
reason, consequence, and any superseded decision or rule. A constitutional
amendment must also include the exact affected constitutional text and its
replacement. Ordinary decisions cannot amend the Constitution.
