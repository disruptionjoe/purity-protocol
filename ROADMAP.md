# Roadmap

This roadmap states intended direction. It is not authority, a promise, an
activation, or evidence that a gate has been satisfied.

## Phase 0 - establish credible initial conditions

Produce one coherent, evidence-supported graduation packet containing:

1. the ratified constitution and authority hierarchy;
2. explicit eligibility rules for effort, decision voice, and
   meta-governance voice;
3. an evidence-gated progressive-decentralization roadmap;
4. constitutional and policy rule tiers with amendment processes;
5. an authority and real-reversibility matrix;
6. consent, exit, fork, and attributable-creation principles;
7. a research agenda covering mechanisms, funding, capture resistance, and
   unresolved rights; and
8. a clearly defined first eligibility-expansion gate.

The packet must distinguish ratified rules, evidence, hypotheses, open
questions, and non-claims. Lane 1 presents it to Joe; the repository cannot
graduate itself.

## Evidence-gated decentralization direction

The intended direction is to expand each of the three eligibility classes only
when the project can metabolize the change without compromising coherence or
governance integrity. Gates should be based on demonstrated capacity and
principle, not elapsed time or arbitrary dates.

Candidate later states may separate:

- broader eligibility to contribute bounded effort;
- broader eligibility to contribute voice to project decisions; and
- broader eligibility to change the rules governing eligibility and voice.

Phase 0 must research the sequence, evidence, stop conditions, and legitimate
transfer method. This section does not establish any state or date.

## Explicitly outside establishment

Protocol mechanics, a Pure App, pilots, contributors, participants, live
incentives, funds, real participant data, deployment, authority transfer, and
Phase 0 graduation are outside the establishment package.
